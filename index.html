Privacy Policy for Hourly Pay Calculator

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

Welcome to the Hourly Pay Calculator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle, process, and protect your information when you visit and use our website.

1. Information We Collect

Because our app is designed to be a fast and secure utility, we keep data collection to an absolute minimum.

  • Information You Provide: The financial data you enter into the calculator (such as company names, hourly rates, regular hours, and overtime hours) is processed locally on your device. We do not store, save, or transmit this financial data to our servers.
  • Automatically Collected Information: Like most websites, we may collect non-personal data automatically when you visit. This includes your browser type, device type, and general usage data to help us ensure the site runs smoothly across different platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used strictly to provide and improve our service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Ensure the calculator functions correctly on your device.
  • Remember your interface preferences (such as your chosen Light, Dark, or Cyberpunk theme, language, and currency settings) using local storage on your browser.
  • Analyze general website traffic and performance to improve the user experience.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage (a standard web technology similar to cookies) to remember your preferences (like language and themes) for your next visit. This ensures you do not have to reset your preferences every time you open the app. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect basic web traffic and error logs necessary for the hosting and performance of the website. We encourage you to review Vercel's privacy policy for more details on how they handle standard web traffic data.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your experience. Because your financial inputs are calculated locally on your device and not stored on external databases, the risk of your personal wage data being exposed is virtually eliminated.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app's features or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
